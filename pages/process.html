How It Works

The Commission
Process

From concept to creation — every step, refined.

01

Reach Out

Start by contacting us via the order form, email, or Instagram DM. Tell us what you're thinking — even rough ideas are welcome. No vision is too big or too small.

📧 Email: sirtajreid@gmail.com
📱 Instagram: @tajmahallicapparel
Or use the commission form on the Order page.

02

Design

We craft custom design concepts built around your idea. Share any references, color preferences, symbols, or text you want incorporated. We go back and forth until it's exactly right.

💡 Tip: The more specific you are about your vision, the faster we can nail it. Bring photos, mood boards, sketches — anything that inspires you.

03

Approve

Final sign-off with a production mockup. You see every detail — placement, color, scale, typography — before we touch a single garment. No surprises, guaranteed.

✓ Full-color digital mockup
✓ Unlimited revisions until you're satisfied
✓ Production does not begin until you approve

04

Delivered

Your custom piece is produced, quality-checked, and shipped directly to you — tracked and insured. We ship worldwide, and we don't rest until the piece meets our standard.

🌍 Worldwide delivery · 📦 Tracked & insured · ⏱ Turnaround: 2–4 weeks depending on complexity
